Fractotum

Procedural Art from Literary Text

Transform your words into stunning visual art and music using pure mathematical algorithms. No AI, no training data, no borrowed creativity — just the mathematical beauty hidden within your text.

✨ See Examples 📁 View Code

How It Works

📖

Text Analysis

Extracts semantic embeddings from your literary text using advanced transformer models, capturing the deep meaning and emotional resonance of your words.

🎨

Visual Generation

Maps semantic features to visual parameters, creating unique fractals with Julia sets, distortion fields, and gravitational dynamics that reflect your text's essence.

🎵

Musical Creation

Transforms the same semantic space into procedural MIDI compositions, creating melodies and harmonies that echo the rhythm and mood of your text.

🔢

Pure Mathematics

No generative AI, no training data, no borrowed creativity. Everything emerges from mathematical transformations, procedural algorithms, and the intrinsic geometry of language.

🎞️

Multiple Formats

Generate static images, animated videos, or MIDI audio files. Each format captures different aspects of your text's mathematical signature.

⚡

Instant Creation

Simply provide a text file and choose your output format. Fractotum handles the rest, delivering unique procedural art in seconds.

Philosophy

Fractotum is an experiment in algorithmic creativity without AI synthesis. Instead of training on existing art or learning from human creativity, it explores how pure mathematics and semantic understanding can generate entirely new forms of beauty.

Every piece is unique to your text, emerging from the mathematical transformation of meaning into visual and musical form. No borrowed aesthetics, no learned patterns — just the hidden geometry of language made visible.
